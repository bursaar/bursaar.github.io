[intro]Thank you for asking to know more about me. If this were a conversation, I'd be quietly flattered. This is a website for my work and personal lives. My film work sits here, along with my photography, writing and blog. [/intro]

[divider]

[dropcap]M[/dropcap]y name is Ben Keenan, I have a lot of interests and skills. If you'd like to know more about my skills, please check out my filmmaking and photography portfolios, if you'd like to know about my interests, please check out my blog.

I'm deeply interested in storytelling - working in film for twelve years and taking photos for six. I write, produce and direct, in that order of commonality. I live in Dublin, Ireland, though I travel as much as I can, I have lived here all my life.

I have produced several short films, including three fully funded projects; Barry's Bespoke Bakery (Irish Film Board), The Chronoscope (Wicklow County Council) and This is Not A Conspiracy Theory (RTÉ). In my late teens and into the start of my twenties, I wrote, directed and co-produced the feature film The Rise of The Bricks, starring Emmett Scanlan, Eoin Macken, Ciaran McNamee, Cillian Scott and Cathal Sheahan.

You can catch my more recent and frequent work over at Half a Giraffe, a comedy project I am developing with some talented folks.

If you would like to use any of my images for anything please contact me. I am very reasonable and obliging!
