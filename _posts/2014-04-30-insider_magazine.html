Good news! I've started writing game (and DVD!) reviews for Insider Magazine, free with the Irish Independent every Thursday. I love writing about games at the best of times, but having my (deftly sub-edited) pieces published nationally is a heady thrill. If you live in Ireland, you can pick up a copy on Thursdays in newsagents and shops across the country.
